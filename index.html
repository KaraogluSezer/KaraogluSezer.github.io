I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Information and Communication Technology section on the TPM faculty at the Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands.

I did my PhD in the Distributed and Embedded Security reserch group under the supervision of Prof dr. Pieter Hartel at the University of Twente, The Netherlands.

My research concerns the security of critical infractructures. In particular, I am interesed in log analysis, network intrusion detection and threat analysis.
